About

In an ideal world, we would all live a comfortable distance from our places of work; for the sake of healthier, happier, and more efficient societies. Unfortunately, this is often not possible. Whether the cause is economic pressure or misfortune, cohabio can help us find a place to live that maximises the time we have with friends and loved ones, at the expense of a hefty commute.

Our algorithms compare thousands of potential home locations based on the minimum expected commuting time for both you and your partner. They take into account your preferred method(s) of transportation, and whether you have a hard limit on the length of time you are willing to travel. Cohabio is the only site that provides this service for any location on planet Earth!
